Music Downloader

Download music from the internet with a simple search is what Music Downloader allows

To download songs with Music Downloader, just enter the name of the artist or the title of the song in the search box and click on the search button.

- Search for the songs you want
- Play and download songs in high quality
- Dark mode
- Can choose the download file format (available formats: M4A, MP3, OPUS)
- Option to change the download directory (by default is in Internal memory, MusicDownload folder)
- Option to view downloads (to find downloaded songs more easily)
- Easy to use
